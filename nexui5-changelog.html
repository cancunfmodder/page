NexUI5 1.1 · Changelog & Known Issues

Final beta snapshot of NexUI5 1.1 for the Samsung A41. Development is discontinued.

Changelog (final beta)
  • Base updated to OneUI 5 for Samsung A41.
  • Integrated OneUI 5.1 launcher for smoother home experience.
  • Clock app from OneUI 5.1.1.
  • Added Zest Menu.
  • Custom boot logo added.
  • Root preinstalled for easier modding.
  • System boots and is usable for basic daily tasks.
  • Wi‑Fi and mobile data functional.
  • Audio playback working (speaker, basic media).
Known issues
  • Bluetooth not working.
  • Camera not working.
  • Flash/torch not working.
  • No further fixes planned (project discontinued).
How to flash
  • 1. Flash Stock ROM.
  • 2. Flash TWRP and VBMETA in AP.
  • 3. Flash NexUI.zip.
  • 4. Flash Kernel.zip.
  • 5. Flash Guard.zip.
  • 6. Format data and reboot.

This ROM is provided as‑is, with no active development. If you decide to flash it, you should be comfortable with the bugs listed above and always keep a backup of your data.
