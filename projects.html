NexUI5 1.1 · Samsung A41
OneUI 5–based custom ROM · Beta · Discontinued

NexUI5 1.1 is a OneUI 5–based ROM for the Samsung A41, with a smoother OneUI 5.1 launcher, OneUI 5.1.1 clock, Zest Menu, custom boot logo, and root preinstalled. Development is discontinued, but the last beta is still available.
